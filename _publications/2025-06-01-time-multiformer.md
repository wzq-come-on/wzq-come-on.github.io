Developed TimeMultiformer for multivariate time series imputation; Introduced time-lag matrix and diagonal masked multi-head attention; Combined Transformer and iTransformer to capture spatio-temporal dependencies; Reduced MAE by 2%–16%, RMSE by 10%–26%, MRE by over 10% on four real datasets.
